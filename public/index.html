How to Play

Goal

Be the first to play all your cards!

On Your Turn

Play a matching card or draw from the deck.

Number cards must match by color or number.

Special Cards

Catnap — Skip next player (colored)
Alley Cat — Reverse direction (colored)
Zoomies (+2) — Next player draws 2 (colored, stacks!)
Copycat (Wild) — Play anytime, pick color
Cat Burglar (Wild +4) — Pick color + next draws 4
Stampede (Wild +6) — Pick color + next draws 6!
Cat-astrophic Pile (Wild +10) — Pick color + next draws 10!!
Purr-ge — Discard all number cards of this color (colored)
Pounce — Steal a random card from someone
Cat Nuke — Skip all other players!
HISS! — Cancel a draw stack

Draw Stacking

All plus cards (+2, +4, +6, +10) stack up. Counter with another +card or HISS!, or draw them all!

Hand Limit

If you ever have more than 25 cards, you're eliminated!

Winning

First to empty their hand finishes 1st. Game continues for remaining players until one is left.
