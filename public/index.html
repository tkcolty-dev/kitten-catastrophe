How to Play

Goal

Be the first to play all your cards!

On Your Turn

Play a matching card or draw from the deck.

Number cards must match by color or number.

Number Cards

Match by color or number. 0-9 in four colors.

Special Cards

Draw Stacking

All plus cards (+2, +4, +6, +10) stack up. Counter with another +card or HISS!, or draw them all!

Hand Limit

If you ever have more than 25 cards, you're eliminated!

Winning

First to empty their hand finishes 1st. Game continues for remaining players until one is left.
